Stigma

This is another educational book that teaches us about HIV/AIDS, its consequences, how we can prevent it and betrayal.

This story talks about two best friends since birth is the university. One of them, Nkiru thought that she had HIV/AIDS when it was her friend Tiwa that really had HIV/AIDS. The betrayal began when Tiwa abandoned Nkiru.
